Who I am.

Hello, I'm John Bettencourt, an aspiring software engineer and dedicated professional currently working in a creative role within a large church. I find immense joy in being a part of creating things that are useful and meaningful to people.

Core Values and Beliefs:

At the core of who I am, I value my family and the relationships I have with my friends. My Christian faith has always been a foundational aspect of my life, guiding my decisions and actions. Now, as I embark on a new career path, I am driven by the desire to provide for my growing family and build a fulfilling life.

Personal Journey

From a young age, I felt a deep passion for software engineering. I started learning Python on my parents' old computer when I was just 12 years old. In college, I followed my calling to work in full-time ministry, which has been an invaluable experience. However, my passion for software engineering has remained constant, and I am now pursuing a career that aligns with my interests and allows me to leverage my skills and creativity.

Passions:

Aside from my software engineering pursuits, I am also a skilled Lighting Designer, merging creativity and technical expertise to craft visually appealing and functional art. These experiences further reinforce my passion for the intersection of technology and creativity. Outside of work, my wife and I have been happily married for a year, and we are excitedly awaiting the arrival of our baby, which fuels my drive to provide a bright future for my growing family.
